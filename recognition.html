Recognition

What others made of it.

The credibility of this archive rests not on its own account of the work, but on the record others have made of it — in scholarship, in criticism, and in the institutions that showed it.

This page is in preparation. The scholarly and critical record is being gathered and verified; the bibliography below is the documented foundation it will be built on.
